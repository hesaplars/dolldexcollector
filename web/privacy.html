DollDex Collector Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

DollDex Collector is an unofficial collection catalog, tracker, and community app.

Data We Process

We process account information, Google sign-in identifiers, email address, profile name, profile photo, unique username, birth-year based age eligibility, collection entries, comments, likes, reports, friend and block relationships, messages, moderation records, subscription status, and app quality data.

How We Use Data

We use data to create and secure accounts, sync collections across devices, provide profiles and social features, moderate reports, prevent abuse, respond to legal requests, verify optional Pro features, and improve app quality.

Age Requirement

DollDex Collector is for users who are at least 13 years old. The app asks for birth year during setup instead of a full date of birth.

Service Providers

The app uses Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Additional services such as billing, ads, analytics, or crash reporting may be added only when configured for release.

Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion inside the app. Account, profile, collection, and related data will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period, unless limited retention is required for safety, fraud prevention, moderation history, payment disputes, or legal obligations.

Unofficial App Notice

DollDex Collector is not affiliated with third-party toy brands. Users are responsible for sharing content that respects copyright, privacy, and community rules.
