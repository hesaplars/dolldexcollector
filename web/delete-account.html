Delete Your DollDex Collector Account

Users can request deletion from inside the app: Profile > Account tools > Delete account request.

What Will Be Deleted Or Anonymized

Profile data, username, collection entries, comments, social relationships, messages, and notification data will be deleted or anonymized where technically and legally appropriate.

What May Be Retained Temporarily

Limited records may be retained when required for security, abuse prevention, moderation history, fraud prevention, payment disputes, or legal compliance.

Processing

Deletion requests are reviewed and processed within a reasonable period after the request is submitted from the signed-in account.
