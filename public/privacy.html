DollDex Collector Privacy Policy

Last updated: To be completed before launch.

DollDex Collector is an unofficial collector catalog and collection tracking app.

Data We Collect

We may collect Google account display name, email address, profile photo, app profile details, collection entries, wishlist data, trade and selling status, comments, reports, moderation records, purchase or subscription status, diagnostics, analytics and advertising identifiers where applicable.

Why We Collect Data

We use data to create and secure accounts, sync collections, show profiles according to privacy choices, provide moderation tools, manage Pro membership, show ads to non-Pro users, improve app quality and fix crashes.

Image URLs

Users and admins may add image URLs. The app displays these URLs as images. Users are responsible for submitting only images they have the right to use.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Some data may be processed by service providers used to run the app, such as Firebase, Google Play Billing, analytics, crash reporting and advertising services.

Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion inside the app and from the public account deletion page. Some limited records may be retained where required for security, abuse prevention, fraud prevention or legal compliance.
