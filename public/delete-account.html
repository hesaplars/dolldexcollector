Delete Your DollDex Collector Account

This page will be completed and published before Google Play release.

What Will Be Deleted

Account profile, collection entries, wishlist entries, trade and selling status, user comments where deletion is legally and technically appropriate, and notification data.

What May Be Retained Temporarily

Some records may be retained for a limited time when required for security, abuse prevention, fraud prevention, legal compliance or purchase verification history.

How To Request Deletion

Option 1: In the app, open Profile, then Account tools, then Delete account request.

Option 2: Send an email from your account email address to the support email below.

Support Email

To be completed before launch.

Processing Time

Deletion requests should be processed within a clearly stated timeframe before launch.
