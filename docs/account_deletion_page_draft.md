# DollDex Collector Account Deletion Page Draft

This page will be published as a public web page before Google Play release.

## Delete Your DollDex Collector Account

You can request deletion of your DollDex Collector account and associated app data.

## What Will Be Deleted

- Account profile
- Collection entries
- Wishlist entries
- Trade and selling status
- User comments where deletion is legally and technically appropriate
- Notification data

## What May Be Retained Temporarily

Some records may be retained for a limited time when required for:

- Security
- Abuse prevention
- Fraud prevention
- Legal compliance
- Purchase/subscription verification history

## How To Request Deletion

Option 1: In the app, open Profile > Account tools > Delete account request.

Option 2: Send an email from your account email address to the support email below.

Support email: To be completed before launch.

## Processing Time

Deletion requests should be processed within a clearly stated timeframe before launch.
