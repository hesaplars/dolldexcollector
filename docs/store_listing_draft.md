# DollDex Collector - Store Listing Draft

## App Name

DollDex Collector

## Short Description

Track doll collections, wishlists, sets, pets and accessories in one clean catalog.

## Full Description

DollDex Collector is an unofficial collection tracker and catalog app for doll collectors.

Build your personal shelf, mark items as owned or wanted, track trade and selling pieces, browse catalog entries, and keep collection details organized across Android and web.

Planned and supported areas include:

- Character and item catalog
- Doll releases
- Sets and concepts
- Pets and accessories
- Image URL previews
- Personal collection tracking
- Wishlist and trade status
- Profiles
- Reports and moderation tools
- Optional Pro membership

Pro membership removes ads and may unlock advanced collection tools.

DollDex Collector is an unofficial fan and collector tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially connected to any toy brand or rights holder.
