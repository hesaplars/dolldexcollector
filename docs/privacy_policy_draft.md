# DollDex Collector Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: To be completed before launch.

DollDex Collector is an unofficial collector catalog and collection tracking app.

## Data We Collect

We may collect:

- Google account display name
- Google account email address
- Google profile photo
- App profile information you choose to add
- Collection entries
- Wishlist, trade and selling status
- Comments, reports and moderation details
- Purchase or subscription status
- App diagnostics, analytics and advertising identifiers where applicable

## Why We Collect Data

We use data to:

- Create and secure your account
- Sync your collection across devices
- Show public profile and collection information based on your privacy choices
- Provide moderation and reporting tools
- Manage Pro membership
- Show ads to non-Pro users
- Improve app quality and fix crashes

## Image URLs

Users and admins may add image URLs. The app displays these URLs as images. Users are responsible for only submitting images they have the right to use. Copyright or inappropriate image reports can be reviewed by admins.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Some data may be processed by service providers used to run the app, such as Firebase, Google Play Billing, analytics, crash reporting and advertising services.

## Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion inside the app and from the public account deletion page. After deletion, account data and collection data will be removed unless limited retention is required for security, abuse prevention or legal reasons.
